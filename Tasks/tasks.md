- Analyze seasonal sales patterns
- Support strategic business expansion decisions

## Task 3: Creating Effective Visuals

Performed data cleaning, transformation, and dashboard development using Power BI.

### Data Cleaning Steps

- Removed records where `Quantity < 1`
- Removed records where `UnitPrice < 0`
- Handled missing customer values
- Created `Revenue` metric using:

```text
Revenue = Quantity × UnitPrice
```

### Dashboard Visuals Created

1. Revenue Trend Analysis
2. Country Performance Analysis
3. Customer Revenue Analysis
4. Global Demand Map

### Skills Applied_2

- Data cleaning
- Data transformation
- Dashboard development
- Business analysis

---

## Task 4: Communicating Insights and Analysis

Presented findings and recommendations to support business decision-making.

### Key Insights

- Revenue shows strong seasonal growth during year-end
- A few countries contribute major international revenue
- High-value customers drive a significant share of sales
- Expansion opportunities exist in high-demand regions

### Recommendations

- Focus expansion on high-performing countries
- Improve customer retention strategies
- Plan inventory based on seasonal demand
- Optimize low-performing products

### Skills Applied_3

- Data storytelling
- Business communication
- Insight presentation
- Strategic recommendation building
